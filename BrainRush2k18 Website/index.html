What is BRAINRUSH ?


“BRAINRUSH” is the annual inter-college game show organised by the Computer Science and Engineering Department of MCKV Institute of Engineering under CSI - MCKVIE Student Chapter Activities. BRAINRUSH promises to test the analytical skills, attentiveness and inquisitiveness of the students. It is a game of utmost intelligence and knowledge, which provides a platform for testing and sharpening the skill-sets of the students. After three very successful runs, this year promises to be more enlighting, illuminating and fun.



Exciting prizes waiting for the winning teams.....

Round Details

*Brain-Den*

  • The first elimination round.
  • How fast you can work in a team, reason your logics and grill your brains to get a solution?
  • Skills checked: Basic Aptitude, Analytical and Logical Reasoning Skills.
  • 35 questions (7 stars), 40 minutes.
  • 20 teams proceed to the next round!

*Hangman*

  • The second elimination round.
  • Fill in the blanks (Guess the word) on the clues you get. The clues may be images or written.
  • Skills checked: Inquisitiveness, Sharpness.
  • 20 questions, 45 minutes.
  • 6 teams proceed to the next round!

*Admataz*

  • The third round.
  • Advertise and sell a product to the judges.
  • Skills checked: Team work.
  • 2 minutes to think, 3 minutes to act.
  • No elimination, all teams proceed to the finals.
  • Scores will be carried forward to the next round.

*The Magic Box*

  • The fourth round.
  • A surprise round for the finale.
  • Skills checked: Strategy, IQ.
